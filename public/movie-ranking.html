Book Recommendations by Money

All of the few books I've read

It ends with us (Colleen Hoover)

a great book if u are a romantic reader. a modern day love story from the pov of girl protagonist.

It starts with us (Colleen Hoover)

sequel of IT ENDS WITH US. Shows the pov of both the guy and the girl.

Ranitatt (Harmanjit Singh)

A punjabi poetic work of the young poet and writer, who is also the author of this book. Basic ideas of the poems are love, culture, heritage, environmental issues, nature.
